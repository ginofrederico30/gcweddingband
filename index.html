SERVICES

WE ARE GOOD COMPANY

Serving Columbus, Ohio and all of Central Ohio

Reception Band

Our 8-piece ensemble includes guitar, keyboards, bass, saxophone, trumpet, drums, and vocals. We specialize in Pop, Soul, Top 40, Motown, Funk, R&B, and Rock.

Cocktail Band

Our 3-piece Jazz Ensemble will play jazz standards during your cocktail hour, starting the evening on a sophisticated and classy note.

Ceremony Essentials

We will facilitate your ceremony with seamless transitions, sound control, and a microphone for the officiant. Choose from a live violin and piano duo, pre-recorded music, or a hybrid blend of both to suit your preferences.

Professional Sound & Lighting

Providing high quality sound and lighting equipment to enhance the music and ambience for your reception, ceremony, speeches, and pretty much anything that needs amplification and lighting.

Polished MCs

Our MC will guide your reception, making key announcements and keeping everything on schedule, while ensuring your guests feel engaged and fully part of the celebration.

Enhance Your Reception

Our live reception band will guarantee the dance floor is packed! With the funkiest song catalog in town, you'll be movin' and groovin' all night long.

Experienced Performers

We've been playing live music together for the past 10 years, from college house parties to sold out shows to dance floors at the most memorable weddings.

The Perfect Playlist

We'll collaborate with you months in advance to curate a customized playlist that captures the essence of your special day!

A Night to Remember

We'll bring your favorite tunes to life, engage with the crowd, and showcase special moments to make your reception the standout party of the year.
