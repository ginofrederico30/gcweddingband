SERVICES

PROVIDING THE BEST

Reception Band

Our 6-7 piece ensemble includes guitar, keyboards, bass, saxophone, trumpet, drums, and vocals. We specialize in Pop, Soul, Top 40, Motown, Funk, R&B, and Rock.

Cocktail Band

Let us set the perfect ambiance with our 2-3 piece jazz band during your cocktail hour. We'll start the evening on a sophisticated and classy note.

Professional Sound and Lighting

Highest quality sound and lighting equipment to enhance the music and ambience for your reception and ceremony, speeches, and pretty much anything that needs amplification and lighting. We've got you covered!

Polished MCs

Our hosts skillfully orchestrate a seamless day, ensuring your guests feel joyous and truly immersed in the celebration. You'll be in good hands!

Enhance Your Reception

Our live reception band will guarantee the dance floor is packed! With the funkiest song catalog in town, you'll be movin' and groovin' all night long.

Experienced Performers

We've been playing live music together for the past 10 years, from college house parties to sold out shows to dance floors at the most memoriable weddings.

The Perfect Playlist

We'll collaborate with you months in advance to curate a customized playlist that captures the essence of your special day!

A Night to Remember
